About Me

My name is Jose Sanchez and I write code for a living. I live and work in Los Angeles, CA. I had the good fortune to graduate from UC Davis with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

After college, I became an IT Specialist where I helped customers from around the USA and Latin America with their tech problems.In addition, I assisted our Sales Engineers with presentations and demos in both Spanish and English. Working in IT helped me to think outside the box and to have a better understanding towards computer users.

I later became a Software Engineer in the television industry. As an engineer doing .NET, I coded software for the use of real-time graphics, and helped craft complex integrations with different TV Networks. It was fun and exciting to work for many of the big media companies and other great television networks (such as NBC, CBS, ESPN, DirecTV, Turner, NFL Network, FormulaOne, etc).

Now, I’m a Full Stack Developer writing iOS apps in Swift and building websites using ReactJS, Node.js, and Go. The world of technology is ever growing, and I'm excited to keep growing with it and to keep learning.
